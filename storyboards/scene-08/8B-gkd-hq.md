# SCENE 8B — GKD HEADQUARTERS, A CORPORATE OFFICE

**Script ref:** `FD3-Script.md` § SCENE 8B
**Characters:** Ji-lan, MAMA, Trubble, Slarth, Zoh-baggo, TK-Maxx
**Location:** GKD corporate office, high-rise. Large windows overlooking the park. Modern office furniture. Sleek, intimidating. 8B is set during the same time-window as 8A (Tony training in the park below).
**Tone:** Hostile, corporate, intimidation. Contrast with 8A's serenity.

## Beat summary

The GKD has captured MAMA and brought her to HQ. Ji-lan demands she sign over the restaurant. She refuses. He force-feeds her sushi. Through the office windows, they spot Tony and Yake-oh training in the park. Ji-lan sends Zoh-baggo and TK-Maxx to deal with them. MAMA is gagged with sushi.
