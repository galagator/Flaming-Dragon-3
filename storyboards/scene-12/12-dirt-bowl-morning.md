# SCENE 12 — DIRT BOWL, THE NEXT DAY

**Script ref:** `FD3-Script.md` § SCENE 12
**Characters:** Jasmine, Tony
**Location:** Inside the Dirt Bowl tent from Scene 11's closing gag. Morning. Daylight leaking through canvas. Rumpled bedding. Intimate two-shot.
**Tone:** Intimate, low-brow romantic comedy. The tent gag from 11-15/16 is now the setup. Dialogue is sincere-ish but the content is on-the-nose.

## Beat summary

Tony and Jasmine are in bed together in the tent the morning after. Jasmine asks if he's sure he wants to do this (go fight GKD at Deaths Dam) and questions whether he can match up to the GKD's persuasive ads. Tony shushes her, declares MAMA is his only true love, and compares Jasmine unfavourably to a moth. Jasmine says she's coming too — white belt in Suq yo Wang. Tony: "Sounds useful. Let's do this!" They commit to the Dam.

## Visual continuity

- **Location:** Inside the tent from Scene 11-15. Canvas walls, daylight leaking through, rumpled bedding. The tent is a Dirt Bowl tent — a regular canvas camping tent, not a romantic yurt. Low-budget authenticity.
- **Lighting:** Soft morning light through canvas. Warm. No hard shadows. The low-key indoor lighting reads as intimate.
- **Wardrobe:**
  - **Tony:** morning-after Tony. Headband may be off or pushed up. Top is off (he's in bed). When the shot frames his chest/shoulders, the headband can be visible nearby.
  - **Jasmine:** red top may be off, may be on — the script doesn't say. The shot list is framed so the rumpled bedding covers what needs covering. Use the red top as the continuity anchor when she's "dressed".
- **Camera style:** Handheld, indie feel. Closer, more intimate than the Dirt Bowl party wide shots from 11. The tent walls make this naturally close.
- **Tone continuity with 11:** the joke continuity is "the tent gag from 11-15 is now sincere morning-after dialogue". The audience gets the payoff in 12-1, then the dialogue plays it for real.
