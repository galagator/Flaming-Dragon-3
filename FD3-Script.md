## SCENE 3 — THE GOONS DESCEND

**Characters:** Tony, MAMA, Trubble, Slarth

Italian restaurant (MAMA's Boy), traditional looking. Italian mum is already in the kitchen.

Tony walks in the door. MAMA is kneading dough.

**TONY:** Hey mamma I'm here!

*MAMA walks out of the kitchen holding a breadboard with the dough. Tony greets MAMA and gives her a kiss on either cheek.*

**MAMA:** Tony, I'm glad you're here. Since your father died it's been so hard to knead the dough with my frail old hands.

*MAMA takes her apron off and puts it on Tony.*

**TONY:** I know MAMA, ever since the… accident… things just haven't been the same.

*Tony looks off in thought. Scene cuts to a memory of Tony's father drowned in a bowl of pasta sauce. Tony snaps back to reality and begins to knead the dough.*

*Trubble clears his throat. Tony and MAMA spin to see Trubble and Slarth looking evil.*

**MAMA:** Hey you two get outta here, I told you we won't sign nothing!

*Tony hides behind MAMA.*

**TRUBBLE:** Oh yes you will.

*Trubble picks up dough and punches it menacingly.*

**SLARTH:** We always get what we want.

*Slarth pushes a fork off the table and Tony sees it happening in slow mo. Tony runs and dives on to the ground to catch the fork falling at the goons' feet.*

**TRUBBLE:** Ha, this guy definitely ain't stopping us.

**SLARTH:** What a flamin joke…

**TRUBBLE:** *(to MAMA)* Our boss is a patient man but his patience is running thin "mama".

**SLARTH:** Yeah, you better watch your step "mama".

**TRUBBLE:** *(to Tony)* Stay out of our way you spineless doughboy.

*Trubble drop kicks dough at Tony which hits him in the face. The goons leave, both stepping over Tony. The door closes and MAMA kneels down next to Tony, breaks down crying.*

**MAMA:** I don't know what I'd do without the restaurant, we've been here for years! Why can't they accept an italian restaurant in chinatown?

**TONY:** *(looks at MAMA, serious voice)* I gotta go mama, I made-ah-da-poopie.

*Tony squaunches out the door holding his ass.*

*MAMA shakes her head, picks up the dough ball, looks at it, and drops her face into it.*

**Characters:** Yake-oh, Erb Dean, Tony, Jasmine, Ji-lan, MAMA, Trubble, Slarth, Zoh-baggo, TK-Maxx

Tony and the crew exit their van to find Ji-lan, his henchmen, and MAMA tied to a chair with a sushi bomb on her chest, gagged with a sushi roll.

**JI-LAN:** Why did it have to come to this? All I wanted was for a Chinatown that was free of any other cultures interfering. It's Chinatown for Christ sake! Is that really so much to ask, not to have a pasta loving Italian Restaurant in china freakin town?? You make me out to be the bad guy but all you have ever done is try to stop my plan of a unified Chinatown from happening.

**TONY:** Chinatown is a place of many cultures! Your racial ideology makes you no better than HITLER!

> *(Flash Donald Trump image)*

**JI-LAN:** There is no reasoning with you people is there!

**MAMA:** What you mean… You people???

**JI-LAN:** That's it, throw her off boys!

*Ji-lan gestures towards MAMA. The goons approach, then the heroes spring to action.*

Tony throws dry spaghetti that sticks in the wall, stopping the goons from throwing MAMA off and buying some time. Ji-lan runs away to a safer spot, dragging MAMA with him.

*All heroes and villains square off:*
- **Tony** vs **Trubble**
- **Yake-oh** vs **Slarth**
- **Erb Dean** vs **TK-Maxx**
- **Jasmine** vs **Zoh-baggo**

Tony defeats Trubble and chases after Ji-lan as the others continue to fight.

Tony approaches Ji-lan who is now cornered near the edge of the dam.

**TONY:** Your time is up Ji-lan, let MAMA go!

**JI-LAN:** SHE HAS A SUSHI BOMB ON HER CHEST THAT IS SET TO EXPLODE!

*Ji-lan gestures to the remote in his hand, then throws away MAMA and proceeds to fight Tony.*

The fight culminates in Tony using the Flaming Dragon Deathblow which stuns the villain. He quickly takes the sushi bomb off MAMA, placing it on the villain, kicking the remote control up into the air and then kicking Ji-lan off the edge of the dam. As Ji-lan falls, Tony catches the remote and detonates the sushi bomb, throwing sushi rolls in every direction.

The goons see their leader finished off and the ones left standing surrender.

*Everyone gets back together and celebrates. A high five between Yake-oh and Tony ends the epic movie.*
